Event venues

Cheong Wa Dae, Gyeongbokgung Palace, Bugaksan Mountain areas and other related places nationwide

To deliver on President-elect Yoon Suk Yeol's key pledge of giving Cheong Wa Dae back to the people, a series of events will celebrate the site's rebirth as an open and symbolic space that reconnects the nation's past and future.

Cheong Wa Dae is being transformed from a symbol of “imperial” power to a relaxing retreat for the people and from a symbol of miscommunication and division to a place of public integration.

Cheong Wa Dae is being transformed from a symbol of royal power to a "relaxing retreat for the people" and from a symbol of miscommunication and division to a "place of public integration."

To celebrate the historic reopening of Cheong Wa Dae to the people and share the hopes and joys of ushering in a new era, the following cultural events below are planned. The wish is for all Koreans to fully and conveniently enjoy all the facilities and scenic views of Cheong Wa Dae.
